# Citation Capital

Citation Capital is a US-based private equity firm that has been active in the pest control industry as part of the broader wave of PE consolidation. Along with GTCR, HCI Equity Partners and Incline Equity Partners, Citation Capital represents the category of specialist mid-market PE platforms that compete against strategic buyers like [[Anticimex]] and [[Rollins Inc]] for independent pest control operator acquisitions in North America. Their presence in the sector contributes to the valuation multiple inflation documented in [[Global Pest Control M&A Multiples 2024-2026]].
