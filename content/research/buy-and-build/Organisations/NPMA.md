# NPMA

The National Pest Management Association (NPMA) is the largest international pest control industry association, headquartered in the United States. [[South African Pest Control Association]] (SAPCA) is affiliated with NPMA, an arrangement that has been instrumental in enabling certification programmes for South African pest control practitioners. NPMA affiliation signals international standards alignment and gives SAPCA members access to global best practice networks and training resources.
