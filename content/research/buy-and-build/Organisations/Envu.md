# Envu

Envu (formerly Bayer Environmental Science) is a professional pest control product manufacturer and chemical supplier that operates as a corporate partner of [[South African Pest Control Association]]. Envu supplies professional-grade pesticides and pest management products to PCOs in South Africa and is one of the key upstream suppliers in the pest control value chain. Its partnership with SAPCA includes support for certification programmes and R&D initiatives. In the context of a roll-up, Envu and similar chemical suppliers represent upstream supplier relationships that a scaled platform operator could use for volume pricing advantages.
