# PCITA

The Pest Control Industries Training Academy (PCITA) is the training arm associated with [[South African Pest Control Association]] (SAPCA). PCITA provides high-quality, industry-standard training programmes at competitive prices, delivered through registered and experienced training facilitators and assessors. Training is accredited by both [[AgriSETA]] and the Department of Education, and completion of relevant courses contributes to the CPD points required for [[DALRRD]] PCO registration renewal. PCITA's training programmes are relevant to a roll-up operator as a staff compliance overhead but also as a potential competitive moat if acquired or exclusively contracted.
