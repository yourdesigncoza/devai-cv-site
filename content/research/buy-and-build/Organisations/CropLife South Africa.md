# CropLife South Africa

CropLife South Africa is a non-profit industry association representing manufacturers, suppliers and distributors of crop protection products, public health solutions and plant biotechnology in South Africa. It plays a bridging role between the chemical supply side of the pest control industry and the regulatory framework administered by [[DALRRD]] under [[Act 36 of 1947]]. CropLife SA has circulated the PCO application form on behalf of DALRRD and provides regulatory guidance and training resources to its members and registered PCOs.
