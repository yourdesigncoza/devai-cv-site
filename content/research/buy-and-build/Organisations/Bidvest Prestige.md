# Bidvest Prestige

Bidvest Prestige is a contract cleaning and specialised cleaning business within [[Bidvest Services International]], itself part of [[Bidvest Group]]. With approximately 48,000 employees, it is described as the largest leading specialist of world-class outsourced solutions in Southern Africa and Mauritius. Its services include contract cleaning, specialised cleaning, training, events staffing, healthcare careworkers and mobile toilet hire. As a sister company to [[Bidvest Steiner]], Bidvest Prestige is relevant to this vault's roll-up analysis because it competes for similar integrated facilities management contracts as independent pest-and-hygiene operators.
