# Rollins Inc

Rollins, Inc. (NYSE: ROL) is the world's second-largest pest control company, dominant in North America, anchored by the Orkin brand. The company pivoted from a diversified conglomerate to a pure-play pest control operator in 1997 and has since compounded growth through continuous bolt-on acquisitions. Key metrics as of early 2026: Q3 2025 quarterly revenue of $1 billion (annualised ~$4bn), 12% year-on-year growth, 25.2% adjusted EBITDA margin, market capitalisation approximately $29 billion. In 2024–2025 Rollins completed over 30 bolt-on acquisitions. The company serves 2.8 million customers from 800+ locations in approximately 70 countries.

**South Africa**: [[Orkin Environmental Services Cape Town]] (orkin.co.za), founded 1980 by brothers Sam and Jack Zinn, is an independent **franchise** of the Orkin brand — not a Rollins company-owned operation. Rollins holds no direct equity in any South African pest control company. Their international strategy is primarily franchise-led outside North America, as distinct from [[Anticimex]]'s acquisition-led international expansion model. This means Rollins is a less likely strategic exit buyer for a SA roll-up platform than either Anticimex or [[Rentokil Initial plc]].

**Global roll-up comparator**: Along with [[Rentokil Initial plc]] and [[Anticimex]], Rollins is one of three global consolidators driving the [[Global Pest Control Consolidation Wave]]. The global pest control market is estimated at ~$26 billion in 2025, with Rollins and Rentokil Initial as the only publicly traded major consolidators.
