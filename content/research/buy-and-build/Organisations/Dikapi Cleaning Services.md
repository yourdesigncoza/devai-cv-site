# Dikapi Cleaning Services

Dikapi Cleaning Services (Pty) Ltd is a Phokeng-based South African cleaning services company that entered a joint venture with [[Rentokil Initial South Africa]] in June 2015 to form [[Rentokil Initial Dikapi]]. The JV was structured to achieve Level 2 BBBEE status with 55.75% black ownership. Little further public information about Dikapi's standalone operations, revenue or CIPC registration details is available from sources reviewed to date (evidence_strength: alleged for most details).
