# Rentokil Initial Dikapi

Rentokil Initial Dikapi is a joint venture formed on 1 June 2015 between [[Rentokil Initial South Africa]] and [[Dikapi Cleaning Services]] (Pty) Ltd, a Phokeng-based cleaning services company. The JV holds a Level 2 BBBEE rating with 55.75% black ownership, enabling it to qualify for state and large-corporate preferred-supplier procurement that requires strong BBBEE credentials. The JV leverages Rentokil's global pest control brand, technology and training against Dikapi's local empowerment credentials and community networks.
