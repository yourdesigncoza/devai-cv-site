# FASA

The Franchise Association of South Africa (FASA) is the industry body representing the franchise sector in South Africa. [[The Specialists]] Franchise Group holds FASA membership, which signals compliance with established franchise governance standards. FASA membership is relevant to a roll-up analysis because it creates a layer of accountability for franchise disclosure documents and operational standards that reduces due diligence risk on franchise acquisitions.
