# EQT

EQT is a Swedish private equity firm that has been an investor in [[Anticimex]] since 2012 (initially EQT VI, then EQT Future from 2021 as a longer-hold vehicle). EQT's value creation model for Anticimex centres on M&A execution, digital transformation (SMART platform), and international expansion. EQT Future's investment thesis specifically includes "international expansion" as a key value driver — suggesting Africa/South Africa may eventually enter Anticimex's expansion roadmap.
