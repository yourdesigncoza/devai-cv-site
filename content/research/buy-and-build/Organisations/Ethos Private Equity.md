# Ethos Private Equity

Ethos Private Equity is a Johannesburg-headquartered South African private equity firm, now operating under The Rohatyn Group (TRG) umbrella. Ethos has historically been one of the most active SA mid-market PE investors, with investments across services, healthcare, financial services and consumer sectors. As one of the largest and most established SA PE platforms, Ethos/TRG represents a credible domestic exit buyer for a SA pest control roll-up that has achieved R100M–R300M revenue with clean BBBEE credentials and documented IFRS financial statements. Their services sector experience would make the pest control buy-and-build model familiar.
