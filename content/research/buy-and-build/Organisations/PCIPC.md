# PCIPC

The Pest Control Industries Professional Council (PCIPC) is an independent non-profit organisation governed by an elected board of trustees and overseen by third-party annual audits. Unlike [[South African Pest Control Association]] (which is a member association), PCIPC operates as a professional body framework, managing the Certificate of Compliance (CoC) process under which 5% of all PCO work is physically audited. PCIPC is pursuing formal recognition via [[SAQA]] (South African Qualifications Authority) as the sector's official professional body. Its role matters to roll-up operators because it creates an additional layer of practitioner accountability beyond company-level SAPCA membership.
