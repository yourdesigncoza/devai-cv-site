# GTCR

GTCR is a Chicago-based private equity firm with a broad portfolio across services, technology and financial services. In the pest control context, GTCR has been identified as one of the PE firms active in pest control roll-up acquisitions, alongside [[Citation Capital]], HCI Equity Partners and Incline Equity Partners. Their participation in the global PE consolidation wave in pest control contributes to the deal volume and multiple expansion documented in [[Global Pest Control M&A Multiples 2024-2026]].
