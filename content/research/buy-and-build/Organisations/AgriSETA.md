# AgriSETA

AgriSETA (Agriculture Sector Education and Training Authority) is the South African government body responsible for skills development in the agriculture sector, including pest control. AgriSETA accredits pest control training programmes and is referenced in [[Act 36 of 1947]] compliance documentation. The Pest Control Training Academy ([[PCITA]]) and other accredited training providers (e.g., Pest Management Academy) submit to AgriSETA oversight. AgriSETA's 2020-2021 sub-sector skills plan for pest control provides industry workforce and training data, noting that Act 36 of 1947 governs the sector and that CPD compliance is managed through [[DALRRD]].
