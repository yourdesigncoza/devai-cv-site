# Bidvest Services International

Bidvest Services International is the corporate services division of [[Bidvest Group]] that houses [[Bidvest Steiner]] (hygiene and pest control), [[Bidvest Prestige]] (contract cleaning and specialised cleaning, ~48,000 workforce), and other service businesses. The division offers a wide range of corporate outsourced services including cleaning, hygiene, waste management, property and facilities management, and security. It operates as the parent structure for Bidvest's commercial services operations across Southern Africa.
