# Bidvest Group

Bidvest Group is a JSE-listed South African multinational conglomerate operating across food services, commercial products, freight, financial services, and facilities/services divisions. Bidvest acquired Steiner Hygiene (now [[Bidvest Steiner]]) in 1991 and owns the pest control-adjacent business via its Bidvest Services International division. In this vault's context, Bidvest Group is a large incumbent holding company whose subsidiary competes directly with independent pest control roll-up targets.
