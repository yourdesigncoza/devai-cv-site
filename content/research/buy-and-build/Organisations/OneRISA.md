# OneRISA

OneRISA is the brand name used by [[Rentokil Initial South Africa]] to market its integrated pest control and hygiene services to the South African commercial market. The OneRISA brand consolidates Rentokil (pest control) and Initial (hygiene/washroom services) under a single service proposition, reflecting the parent company Rentokil Initial plc's strategy of selling bundled pest and hygiene contracts to commercial clients. In the SA context, OneRISA branding is used primarily for large commercial and corporate accounts where clients prefer a single vendor for pest management and washroom hygiene.
