# Synvita

Synvita is a South African manufacturer of professional pest control products headquartered in Pietermaritzburg, KwaZulu-Natal. The company produces product lines for cockroach control, fly control, and rodent control, targeting households, agriculture, public health institutions, offices, corporate and industrial facilities. Synvita is an upstream chemical supplier to the pest control industry rather than a service operator. The company offers registered PCOs preferential pricing for online product orders. In the context of a roll-up, Synvita and similar domestic manufacturers (alongside international suppliers like [[Envu]]) represent potential volume pricing relationships that a scaled platform operator could negotiate.
