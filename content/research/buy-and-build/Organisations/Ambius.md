# Ambius

Ambius is an interior landscaping and sensory branding services business that is part of the Rentokil Initial Group. Ambius offers indoor plants, scenting, artwork and holiday décor services to commercial clients. In South Africa, Ambius operates as part of the [[Rentokil Initial South Africa]] group alongside the pest control (Rentokil) and hygiene (Initial) divisions. From the perspective of a SA pest control roll-up, Ambius is not a competitor but illustrates Rentokil Initial's strategy of providing a bundled suite of workplace services to large commercial clients.
