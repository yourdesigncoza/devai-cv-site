# Bambanani Pest Control

Bambanani Pest Control is a South African BEE-structured pest control and hygiene business that operates as a subsidiary of [[Flick Services Group]]. Originally known as Amalgamated Pest Industries, the business was purchased by Flick Pest Control in April 1995 and renamed Bambanani Pest Control in 1999. It was established specifically as a BEE company, separate to Flick Environmental Services, with both a hygiene division and a pest control division. Bambanani was formally consolidated into [[Flick Services Group]] on 1 September 2023. Its services cover commercial, retail, industrial and domestic markets.
