# The Specialists

The Specialists Franchise Group is one of South Africa's most significant mid-tier pest control and cleaning operators, having operated for approximately 46 years (founded c.1978). The group operates through a franchise model under two primary brands: Pest Control Specialists™ (pest control) and Cleaning Specialists™ (commercial and domestic cleaning and hygiene). With over 65 individual franchises across South Africa, Botswana, Namibia, Swaziland and Zambia, The Specialists has built the broadest geographic footprint of any independently branded pest control operator in southern Africa outside the three large incumbent operators.

The franchise model distinguishes The Specialists structurally from both the Tier 1 integrated operators ([[Bidvest Steiner]], [[Servest]], [[Rentokil Initial South Africa]]) and the fragmented sole-trader Tier 3. Each franchise unit is independently owned and operated, with The Specialists Franchise Group serving as the brand licensor, standards enforcer, and supply chain aggregator. Membership in [[FASA]] (Franchise Association of South Africa) indicates compliance with formal franchise industry standards. The SAPCA and NCCA dual memberships create cross-selling opportunities within each franchise unit across pest control and commercial cleaning.

From a roll-up perspective, The Specialists occupies an ambiguous position. On one hand, the franchise network of 65+ units represents a substantial installed base of operator relationships — acquiring the franchisor entity or a portfolio of individual franchises could be the fastest path to meaningful scale. On the other hand, the franchise model implies limited direct control over individual unit operations, customer contracts, and financial performance, making a bolt-on acquisition of the group complex. Individual mature franchises that are approaching owner retirement or succession challenges may be cleaner acquisition targets than the group entity.

The company's ~46 years of operation, brand recognition ("one of Southern Africa's most reliable names in pest control"), and multi-country presence make it a strategic asset. It is not publicly traded and its ultimate beneficial owners are not publicly disclosed; revenue and EBITDA are undisclosed.
