# Buy-and-Build: SA Pest Control Roll-Up

> [!abstract] Research Goal
> Map the South African pest control industry as a buy-and-build / roll-up opportunity: market structure, acquisition targets, regulation, unit economics, competitive landscape, and exit pathways from 2024-2026.

## Seed entities (round 1)
- [[Government/DALRRD]] — National regulator of SA pest control operators and pesticides under Act 36 of 1947; registers all PCOs.
- [[MarketSegments/Food Handling Market Segment]] — Most valuable pest control segment; non-discretionary HACCP-driven spend; very high stickiness; top roll-up priority.
- [[MarketSegments/South African Pest Control Market]] — SA pest control ~USD 198M (2025); CAGR 7.1%; fragmented; 3 large incumbents; prime roll-up conditions.
- [[Organisations/Bidvest Steiner]] — Bidvest's hygiene arm; founded 1954; 120k+ customers; pest control bundled into hygiene rental offering.
- [[Organisations/Rentokil Initial South Africa]] — SA subsidiary of world's largest pest control company; 12 branches; operates since 1967 under OneRISA brand.
- [[Organisations/Servest]] — Africa's first black-owned FM company; 5,000+ clients; KTH-backed; pest control is part of FM bundle.
- [[Organisations/South African Pest Control Association]] — SA pest control industry body, est. 1964; government-commissioned under Act 36 of 1947; NPMA-affiliated.
- [[Regulations/Act 36 of 1947]] — SA's primary PCO/pesticide law; requires registration for all who apply pesticides for reward; operated since 1947.
- [[Regulations/HACCP]] — Food safety standard requiring mandatory pest control; drives non-negotiable recurring revenue in food sector.

## MarketSegments (researched — rounds 2–5)
- [[MarketSegments/BBBEE Competitive Advantage]] — BBBEE Level 1/2 gives 135%/125% procurement recognition; mandatory in govt tenders; structural moat for compliant operators.

- [[MarketSegments/General Commercial Market Segment]] — Office/retail/industrial pest control; OHS-driven recurring contracts; good margins; high stickiness at site level.
- [[MarketSegments/Healthcare and Institutional Market Segment]] — Government health facility pest control: 3-year DoH tenders, 90/10 PPPFA scoring, structural PCO cert + BBBEE mandatory.
