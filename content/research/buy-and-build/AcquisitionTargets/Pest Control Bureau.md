# Pest Control Bureau

Pest Control Bureau is a pest control company established in 1928, making it one of the oldest — possibly the oldest — formally documented pest control operators in South Africa. The business has been serving Johannesburg homeowners, businesses and residential builders continuously from its founding and remains locally owned and operated, based at 279 Pendoring Road, Northcliff, Johannesburg (telephone: 011 478-2058). Technicians hold SAPCA membership and DALRRD registration. The company uses only DALRRD-registered, environmentally sensitive products and offers a full range of pest control inspections, treatment services and termite treatment programmes.

From a roll-up acquisition standpoint, Pest Control Bureau is among the highest-probability exit candidates in the Gauteng market. A business that has operated under local private ownership for nearly 100 years presents near-certain succession dynamics: the founding family or current owners are statistically very likely in succession-stage ownership. A 95-year-old locally-owned PCO carries significant intangible value beyond its financial metrics: established brand recognition in the Northcliff/Johannesburg market, entrenched route density among long-term residential and commercial clients, and demonstrable technician retention (operations of this age cannot persist without stable workforce practices).

The termite treatment specialisation is strategically notable. Termite control in Johannesburg requires pre-construction and post-construction treatment expertise and is more technically demanding than standard pest management. Termite contracts tend to be higher-value, warranty-based agreements with construction companies and property developers — a fundamentally different and more valuable contract type than routine monthly pest control. Pest Control Bureau's 95-year operational history likely includes longstanding relationships with established Johannesburg construction and property management firms that would be difficult to replicate.
