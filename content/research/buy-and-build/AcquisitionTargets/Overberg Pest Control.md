# Overberg Pest Control

Overberg Pest Control cc (OPC) is a Western Cape fumigation specialist established in 1999 and registered with both DALRRD and [[South African Pest Control Association]] (SAPCA). The company specialises in two distinct service categories: (1) Structural Fumigations — buildings, houses, warehouses and structures; and (2) Agricultural Post-Harvest Fumigations — commodities such as stored agricultural product including grain and processed products in bulk bins, trucks, trains, warehouses and silos. OPC holds fumigation-field DALRRD registration for both phosphine gas (aluminium phosphide) and ProFume (sulfuryl fluoride), the latter being a more advanced precision fumigant increasingly replacing methyl bromide globally.

As a specialist fumigation operator rather than a full-service PCO, Overberg Pest Control occupies a distinct niche in the SA acquisition target universe. It represents the type of operator that would add the agricultural/post-harvest fumigation capability to a roll-up platform — a capability that most Tier 3 bolt-ons (who focus on structural pest control) cannot offer. The dual structural + agricultural fumigation specialisation, combined with DALRRD registration in both fields, positions OPC above a simple bolt-on in strategic value.

The Western Cape location gives access to the agricultural export logistics chain: fruit, wine and grain exports through Cape Town harbour and Saldanha Bay. Agricultural clients in the Overberg region (wheat, canola, deciduous fruit) and the Garden Route (dairy, vegetable growing) would represent stable commercial fumigation contracts tied to commodity export seasons.
