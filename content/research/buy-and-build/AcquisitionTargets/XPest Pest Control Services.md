# XPest Pest Control Services

XPest Pest Control Services (also trading as XPest Control Solutions) is a family business based in Pretoria and Johannesburg, established in 1986 with nearly 40 years of continuous Gauteng operation. The company's principal owner Tony has been personally involved in the pest control industry since 1971 — over 55 years of industry experience — making XPest one of the oldest active independent PCOs in Pretoria. Tony would now be approximately 70–80 years old, representing a near-certainty of succession-stage ownership and likely an active interest in exploring a liquidity event. XPest is registered with SAPCA and operates strictly per DALRRD requirements.

The company covers the full Gauteng metro: Pretoria, Centurion, Midrand, Pretoria North, Sandton, Fourways, Kempton Park, Benoni, Alberton, Johannesburg, Randburg, Bryanston and Bedfordview — one of the most comprehensive area coverage footprints of any independent Gauteng PCO documented in this vault. The company offers full food safety standard HACCP documentation and rodent treatment programmes compliant with audit requirements. BBBEE compliance is explicitly noted ("endeavours to incorporate Black Economic Empowerment Groups and remain BEE compliant"). Technicians attend international pest control conferences and HACCP training. A 5-year termite treatment guarantee is offered.
