# NSS Environmental Solutions

NSS Environmental Solutions is a pest control company registered in Durban, South Africa with CIPC registration number 2009/014820/23, incorporated in 2009. The company is based at 4 Yaleside Gardens, Caneside, eThekwini, 4068, KwaZulu-Natal (telephone: +27 82 294 9002) and has been operating for over 10 years. Technicians hold DALRRD registration (historically under the former Department of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries) with a reported 15 years of technician-level experience in the pest control industry. The business provides residential and commercial pest control services.

NSS Environmental Solutions is one of the relatively few SA acquisition targets for which a verifiable CIPC company registration number is publicly known (2009/014820/23). This provides a concrete starting point for formal due diligence: a CIPC lookup can confirm the current company status, registered directors and registered address, while SARS eFiling records will confirm tax compliance status. The eThekwini/Durban base positions NSS as a KZN bolt-on complementary to [[Tidal Pest Control]] (based in Ballito, KZN North Coast) — two KZN operators together would begin to build geographic route density within the province's primary urban market.

The naming convention "Environmental Solutions" is notable in the SA pest control context — it signals a positioning around integrated pest management and environmental responsibility rather than pure chemical treatment. This positioning is commercially relevant for food processing, hospitality and healthcare clients who increasingly specify eco-friendly or HACCP-aligned pest management in tender requirements.
