# Orkin Environmental Services Cape Town

Orkin Environmental Services Cape Town is a South African pest control company operating as an independent franchise under the Orkin brand (owned by Rollins Inc.), founded in 1980 by brothers Sam and Jack Zinn as a pest and wood borer control specialist. The business is based in Cape Town, Western Cape. It is important to note that this entity is **not a Rollins Inc. company-owned operation** — Orkin Global franchises its brand internationally and the Zinn brothers' company is one such franchisee. Rollins/Orkin has no disclosed company-owned operations or consolidation platform in South Africa.

Orkin Cape Town has achieved a level of industry credibility that places it above the typical Tier 3 bolt-on: the business "has worked on national executive committees for the [[South African Pest Control Association]] (SAPCA)," indicating leadership-level engagement in the industry body. Service offerings include pest management, washroom hygiene, specialised disinfections/sanitations/fumigations, woodborer/termite and rot control (including heritage buildings and national monuments restoration), and HACCP-based pest control with quality assurance. The business employs qualified Environmental Health Practitioners (EHPs) registered with the Health Professions Council of South Africa — a significantly higher qualification standard than standard DALRRD PCO registration.

**Acquisition complexity**: Any acquisition of Orkin Cape Town would require resolution of the Orkin Global franchise agreement. Options include: (1) negotiate a franchise exit fee and rebrand the acquired entity under a roll-up platform brand; (2) maintain the franchise relationship and continue operating as Orkin (subject to Rollins/Orkin Global approval); or (3) approach Orkin Global/Rollins as a co-investor or exit pathway if they wish to transition the franchise to a company-owned operation. The franchise complication is not insurmountable but adds 60–90 days of legal and commercial structuring.

**Succession signal**: Founded in 1980 by Sam and Jack Zinn — the founders would now be in their late 60s to early 80s, making this among the highest-probability succession-exit candidates in the SA market alongside [[Pest Control Bureau]] (founded 1928).
