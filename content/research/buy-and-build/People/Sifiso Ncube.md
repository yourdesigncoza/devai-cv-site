# Sifiso Ncube

Sifiso Ncube serves as Vice President of the South African Pest Control Association ([[South African Pest Control Association]]), the primary industry body for SA pest control. His background is in facilities management and commercial cleaning sectors — specifically, he holds (or has held) the role of Executive Chairman at Kusile Facilities Management and is associated with Kusile Financial Services, Summit Life and Summit Funeral Services under a group executive chairman role.

Ncube's appointment as SAPCA VP with an FM background is strategically noteworthy for two reasons. First, it signals a deliberate connection between the pest control industry and the broader SA facilities management (FM) sector — the same sector that provides the parent context for Tier 1 SA competitors [[Bidvest Steiner]], [[Servest]] and [[Flick Services Group]]. Second, an FM executive's presence at the SAPCA leadership level suggests ongoing industry dialogue about convergence between pest control and integrated FM services, which is the structural direction the SA roll-up's eventual exit buyers (including [[Rentokil Initial plc]]) operate within.
