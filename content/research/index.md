*April 2026 — 74 notes, 13 iterations, 14 named acquisition targets*

South Africa's pest control market is fragmented (1,000+ formal operators), regulatory-moated (individual DALRRD registration under Act 36 of 1947), and uncontested by any global consolidator as of April 2026. The vault maps the full investment thesis: market structure, regulatory framework, unit economics, BBBEE structuring requirements, and a named acquisition target universe across five provinces.
