# Open questions

Things I'm chewing on but haven't resolved into a [[decisions/|decision]] yet. First-class content — worth capturing while the thinking is live, even when (especially when) I can't yet answer them.

An open question might sit here for a day, a year, or indefinitely. When one resolves, it graduates into `decisions/` with a link back. When one stops mattering, it stays here as a record of what I was thinking.

Inspired by the [[projects/signaltrace-site|signaltrace]] convention of keeping open questions as a sibling folder to content rather than as footnotes inside it.
