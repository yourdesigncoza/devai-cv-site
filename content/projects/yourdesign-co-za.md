# yourdesign.co.za

> The original YourDesign site — 20+ years of freelance WordPress, WooCommerce, and custom PHP work for small-to-medium businesses and agency sub-contracts. Still the record of where I started.

**Stack:** WordPress, WooCommerce, PHP / MySQL, LAMP on Ubuntu, jQuery / JavaScript, HTML5 / CSS / SASS, Bootstrap / Foundation, custom child themes on GeneratePress.
**Status:** Maintained as portfolio / contact surface.
**Live:** [yourdesign.co.za](https://yourdesign.co.za/)

## The story

yourdesign.co.za is the long tail. Before the quant work and the agent pipelines there were 20+ years of web development, most of it in WordPress and WooCommerce, delivered solo or as a lead developer on agency sub-contracts. The site still positions me as a freelance web developer and still takes client enquiries.

The named work on the site is the heavier end of that period: **Nimue Skin Technology** (brand build), the **Woolworths Mother's Day gift-carding campaign** (via Flume Digital Agency as lead developer), and a longer tail of client work including contributions from Durc (eee.co.za), Pierre Potgieter (Novumintelligence), and Tammy (41Sloane). The stack the site advertises is deliberate: custom child themes, WooCommerce custom functions, advanced custom fields, custom post types, plugin development. Page builders and premium themes are not what goes on a custom build.

The through-line that matters for the rest of this CV is on the 2023 one-page cover letter: *"I started my programming journey coding 'bots' for a trading company way back in the early 2000s, before embracing the Open Source community developing in PHP MySql."* That loop — trading bots, then two decades of web development, back to trading systems via AI — is why the current Eden Fintech work didn't feel like a pivot.
