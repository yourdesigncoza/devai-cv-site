### XAU/USD Trading Analysis

### 4 Hour Chart Analysis

Upon examining the 1-hour chart of XAU/USD, we can identify key price action levels and potential trading opportunities. Let's break it down according to ICT principles.

- **Previous Highs and Lows**: We see that the price made a high at around 2434.94 and then retraced sharply to 2353.44 before rallying again. This indicates a potential liquidity grab above 2434.94.
- **Order Blocks**: The sharp move down to 2353.44 could signify a mitigation of an order block. Typically, we look for previous demand areas that have caused significant moves, which can be potential reversal points.
- **Fair Value Gaps**: The drop from 2431.06 to 2353.44 likely created a fair value gap. The market often retraces into these gaps before continuing in the original direction.

### 15 Minute Chart Analysis

- **Market Structure Shifts**: The sharp rally post-retracement suggests a market structure shift. We should look for a break in market structure at lower time frames to confirm bullish intent.
- **Optimal Trade Entry (OTE)**: Measure the recent swing high and low to find the 62-79% retracement zone, which often aligns with a premium or discount area for an optimal trade entry.

### Refined Trading Suggestions

#### Bullish Scenario:

- **Entry**: Wait for a retracement into the fair value gap created by the drop from 2431.06 to 2353.44.
- **Confirmation**: Look for bullish market structure shifts on the 15-minute chart. Ideally, price should hold above the 2400 level.
- **Target**: First target at 2431.06 and then potentially higher towards 2434.94.

#### Bearish Scenario:

- **Entry**: If the price fails to hold above 2400 and breaks below recent lows, consider shorting.
- **Confirmation**: Look for bearish order blocks and continuation patterns on lower time frames.
- **Target**: 2353.44, with an extended target towards the previous low at 2350 level.

### Immediate Actionable Insights

- **Current Position**: The price is trading around 2419.22, which is relatively close to the recent high.
- **Watch Levels**: Key levels to watch are 2431.06 and 2434.94 for resistance and 2400 for support.
- **Trade Setup**:
	- For a bullish trade, a retracement into the fair value gap with confirmation on the 15-minute chart is ideal.
		- For a bearish trade, a failure to hold above 2400 with a break below recent lows could be a good setup.

### Summary

Based on ICT trading principles, focus on key support and resistance levels, watch for market structure shifts, and look for optimal trade entry zones for high-probability setups. Keep an eye on the 15-minute chart for confirmation before entering trades.
